Job Description:

JSDSolutions is seeking an Automotive Test Engineer to support our premium vehicle software testing team in Krakow.

Your responsibilities:
- Execute software and system-level testing for ECU components in premium automotive vehicles
- Validate diagnostic jobs and DTC (Diagnostic Trouble Code) behaviour using established automotive diagnostic protocols
- Act as the interface between the customer and the R&D development team for issue resolution
- Reproduce, analyse, and document software defects in the ECU systems
- Execute regression testing following software releases
- Maintain test documentation and traceability records
- Use Jira for defect management and project tracking
- Participate in continuous improvement initiatives to enhance test process efficiency

Your profile:
- Engineering degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or Automotive Engineering
- 2+ years of experience in automotive software testing
- Knowledge of automotive ECU diagnostics and diagnostic protocols
- Experience with CANoe, CANalyzer, or equivalent diagnostic analysis tools
- Familiarity with CAN, LIN, or Ethernet-based automotive communication protocols
- Experience with test management tools
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
- English fluency required; German is a plus
- Willingness to collaborate with international teams

We offer:

- Work permit assistance for non-EU candidates
- Private healthcare

Job Description:

We are looking for a Software Test and Validation Engineer to join our Automotive Embedded Systems team in Wroclaw.

Your responsibilities:
- Design and execute software test cases for embedded automotive ECU systems
- Perform validation and verification activities within an ASPICE-aligned V-model development process
- Execute system-level and integration testing for ECU software components
- Analyse test results, identify defects, and track them through the full defect lifecycle
- Work with UDS (ISO 14229) and DoIP (ISO 13400) diagnostic protocols for ECU validation
- Use diagnostic tools to validate ECU behaviour and DTC handling
- Maintain traceability matrices and test documentation
- Create test reports and KPI dashboards for project managers and stakeholders
- Collaborate with software developers and system architects to improve product quality

Your profile:
- Degree in Computer Science, Electronics, Automotive Engineering or similar
- 2+ years experience in embedded software testing or automotive validation
- Knowledge of AUTOSAR architecture (Classic or Adaptive)
- Experience with UDS diagnostic protocol and ECU diagnostics
- Familiarity with ASPICE processes and V-model development lifecycle
- Experience with test management tools (Test Guide, ALM, or similar)
- Proficiency with Jira for defect tracking and test management
- Python or scripting skills for test automation are a plus
- Experience with Wireshark, DLT Viewer, or similar diagnostic analysis tools is advantageous
- Fluent English; German is a plus

We offer:
